Rick Sanchez

A Note on Rick

Rick Sanchez is one of the main characters on a popular Adult Swim animated television series Rick and Morty. He is easily the smartest entity in the entire universe. Although he is a genius, he is also an alcoholic and reckless.


Biography

Rick is a 70 year old human that works as a scientist, inventor, and an arms salesman. He has a daughter named Beth and two grandchildren named Summer and Morty. He once had a wife, but she died due to a terrible accident.

Appearance

Rick is a tall, lanky old man. He has long legs and arms and is very skinny. He has a dimly tanned ashy complexion and grey-blue hair with a bald spot on the back of his head. He has spiky hair on his head and a unibrow. He wears a white lab coat with a light blue green shirt underneath. He also wears brown pants, a dark brown belt with a yellow buckle, and black shoes. He is occasionally seen with some green spill on his mouth, Showing up mainly when he is drunk or after he throws up.

Personality

Rick is sociopathic, emotionally abusive, dismissive, narcissistic, self-centered, incredibly intelligent, and an alcoholic mad scientist who often drags his 14 year old grandson, Morty, and sometimes other members of his family on dangerous adventures.

Relationships

Though exploitative and demanding, Rick genuinely cares about his grandson and values their adventures together, possibly making him the only Rick in existence to do so. He hesitates to outwardly show any love or appreciation however, making sure Morty doesn't get cocky.Rick initially cared very little for and paid scarce attention to his granddaughter, saying her opinion meant "very little" to him. As time went on, however, the two began to bond and go on their own adventures, making her, in a sense, Rick's second sidekick. It appears that Rick loves his daughter, and for the most part is kind to her. Rick also treats Beth with more respect than he does the rest of the family.
